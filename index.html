About

Mixing and mastering are the final steps in delivering your music to the world.
Let's get it right!

No matter how or where your tracks were recorded, I can work with you to produce the best sounding masters ready for streaming or physicals like CD, tape or vinyl.

If you have any queries or concerns along the way I’m always around to chat through your project and make sure we get your record sounding exactly how you want it.

  • Recording, Mixing and mastering engineer based in Kent, UK
  • Over 17 years' studio experience
  • Numerous productions on BBC radio, BBC 6Music, Radio X, Kerrang + more
  • Quick turnaround (Efficent, not rushed!)
  • I specialise in alternative things but honestly, if there is passion I'm here for it
  • Happy to discuss creative ideas and requirements as much as you need
  • Unlimited revisions; we're not done until everyone's happy (with the record)
